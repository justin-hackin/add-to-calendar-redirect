Privacy Policy for Calendar Chooser Extension

Last Updated: December 4, 2024

Overview

The Calendar Chooser Extension is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension redirects Google Calendar "Add to Calendar" links to a configurable handler URL.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. This extension operates entirely locally in your browser.

Data Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage to store only user preferences (redirect URL and enabled/disabled state). This data is stored locally and never transmitted to external servers.

Permissions Used

  • storage: To save your settings
  • webNavigation: To intercept Google Calendar links
  • tabs: To open the settings page
  • contextMenus: To provide context menu options

How It Works

All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to our servers or any third-party servers.

Contact

If you have questions, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
